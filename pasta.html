White Sauce Pasta

Preparation Time: 25 mins

Difficulty: Easy

White Sauce Pasta is a creamy and delicious dish made with soft pasta coated in a rich and cheesy white sauce. It is perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook until soft (al dente).
  3. Drain the pasta and keep it aside.
  4. In a pan, melt butter and add a little flour to make a base.
  5. Slowly add milk while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  7. Add cheese, salt, and pepper to the sauce.
  8. Add boiled pasta into the sauce and mix well.
  9. Cook for 2–3 minutes and serve hot.
