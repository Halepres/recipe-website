Fried Rice

Preparation Time: 20 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Fried Rice is a quick and flavorful dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, sauces, and spices. It is perfect for using leftover rice.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cook rice beforehand and let it cool completely.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  3. Add chopped garlic and sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add vegetables like carrots, beans, and capsicum.
  5. Stir-fry the vegetables on high flame.
  6. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  7. Add cooked rice to the pan.
  8. Mix everything well without breaking the rice.
  9. Cook for 2–3 minutes and serve hot.
