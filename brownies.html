Chocolate Brownies

Preparation Time: 40 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Chocolate Brownies are soft, rich, and fudgy desserts made with cocoa and chocolate. They are perfect for dessert lovers and easy to bake at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. In a bowl, melt butter and mix it with sugar.
  3. Add eggs and mix well until smooth.
  4. Add cocoa powder and flour and mix properly.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking tray.
  6. Spread evenly using a spatula.
  7. Bake for 20–25 minutes.
  8. Let it cool completely before cutting.
  9. Cut into pieces and serve.
