Chilli Potato

Preparation Time: 30 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Chilli Potato is a crispy and spicy Indo-Chinese snack made with fried potatoes tossed in flavorful sauces and vegetables. It is perfect as a quick snack or party starter.

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 onion
  • 1 capsicum
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chilli sauce

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the potatoes, then cut them into thin long strips like fries.
  2. Soak the potato strips in cold water for 10–15 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain the water and pat the potatoes dry using a clean cloth.
  4. Coat the potatoes with cornflour and a little salt evenly.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the potatoes until they turn golden and crispy.
  6. Remove and place them on tissue paper to absorb excess oil.
  7. In another pan, heat a little oil and sauté chopped onions and capsicum.
  8. Add soy sauce, chilli sauce, and tomato ketchup and mix well.
  9. Add the fried potatoes to the sauce and toss properly.
  10. Cook for 2–3 minutes and serve hot.
