Kadhai Paneer

Preparation Time: 35 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Kadhai Paneer is a popular Indian dish made with paneer cooked in a spicy and tangy tomato-based gravy along with onions and capsicum.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer
  • 1 onion
  • 1 capsicum
  • Tomato puree
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cut paneer into cubes and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions and capsicum.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add spices like turmeric, chilli powder, and garam masala.
  6. Mix everything well and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add paneer cubes and mix gently.
  8. Add a little water if needed and cook for 5–7 minutes.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
